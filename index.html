What You Will Find on ETHOS

ETHOS consolidates eight key capabilities designed to support MCMC staff in understanding, adopting, and governing emerging technologies responsibly.

01In-Prompt-To-U

Practical AI training programmes to help staff create visuals, agents, videos, and digital content with confidence.
02Emerging Tech News Highlight

Curated updates on the latest developments across AI, robotics, quantum, cybersecurity, and other emerging technology domains.
03Success Stories

Real-world use cases showing how emerging technologies are adopted across MCMC divisions and internal initiatives.
04ETx Toolkits

Quick-access practical tools including Act Navigator, ETx Copilot, templates, guides, and emerging technology references.
05AI Gen. Frontier Podcast

GenAI-powered podcast content featuring expert perspectives and monthly commentary on emerging technology topics.
06Game Zone

Gamified learning experiences that make emerging technology concepts accessible through interactive mini-games.
07Ethics & Governance

Frameworks for responsible technology adoption covering fairness, transparency, accountability, and societal impact.
08Expression of Interest

A collaboration channel for staff to propose ideas, co-develop solutions, and work with ETAD on emerging technology initiatives.
